  • The Expanse Series

    The world of The Expanse will not seem all that different from the world of today. People work to support themselves and their families. Governments fight to maintain power, influence, and resources. One difference however is that much of these things happen not on Earth but spread throughout the solar system. Explore what may come in the future of humanity. And see how an unexpected discovery changes it all forever.

  • Calypso

    In Calypso, David Sedaris explores themes about time, aging, family, and mortality. He recounts stories of spending time at his family beach house on the North Carolina coast, how he became a FitBit addict, and how he fed his removed tumor to a snapping turtle. Calypso will of course make you laugh until your sides ache but Sedaris also knows how to make you tear up in other ways.

  • Good Omens

    Have you ever wondered what would happen if the Antichrist was raised by a lovely family in the English countryside? Or how Earth's liasons from heaven and hell, who quite like their earthly lives, would go about preventing the rapture? Wonder no more. Good Omens has all of the answers for you.

Follow Arthur as he escapes Earth with his alien friend Ford Prefect and they explore the galaxy.

About the library

Libraries are essential establishments in practically every city, and Practicum's library is no exception. It first opened its doors way back in 2018, right as our humble town began bidding welcome to its very first citizens!

Since its initial opening, the library has seen nearly 342 visitors each month. That's almost 50% of our population! So far, most citizens have enjoyed reading books and making use of our ample online resources.

The library gets its name from Joan Feynman, an American astrophysicist and the first woman to be elected as an officer of the American Geophysical Union.
